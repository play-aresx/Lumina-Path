We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or non-personal information. Specifically, the App does not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information

The App is designed to be safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. We comply with the Google Play Families Policy and the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

The App does not store user data. All functionality occurs entirely on the user’s device, ensuring maximum privacy and security.
